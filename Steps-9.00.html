Data Transformation
 
In this step you will clean and transform the data:
1. Select Transform Data from the Home tab
2. Change the year column to ascending 
3. Uncheck null for price p/u column
4. Change Column name Value to Total Sales 
5. Uncheck the Sum of Others for the county column. 
6. Replace value from San Luis Obisp to San Luis Obispo and San Luis Obispoo to San Luis Obispo. 
7. Replace the value from ton to TONS for a Unit column.
